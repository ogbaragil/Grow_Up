Implemented flexible cashflow recurrence:
- One-off
- Weekly
- Fortnightly
- Monthly
- Quarterly
- Yearly

Logic:
- Money In / Money Out monthly totals convert recurring items into monthly equivalents.
- Next 7 Days expands recurring items based on their actual frequency.
- Transaction editor now has Frequency dropdown.
- frequency is stored on transaction objects.
- recurring is derived from frequency !== oneOff.

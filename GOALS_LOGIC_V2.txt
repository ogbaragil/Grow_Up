Implemented Goals v2:
- Three goal types:
  1. Net Worth Goal
  2. Account Growth Goal
  3. Debt Payoff Goal

Logic:
- Net Worth Goal automatically uses selected month net worth.
- Account Growth Goal can link to an asset account and uses that selected month balance.
- Debt Payoff Goal can link to a debt account and uses starting debt minus current debt.
- Manual override is available when no account is linked.
- Monthly amount needed is calculated from remaining amount and deadline.
- Existing old goals are inferred where possible for backward compatibility.
